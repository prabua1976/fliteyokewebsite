About Us

Every time we hear the sound of an aircraft engine, we look up as if it is the first time we see an airplane.

We are a father and son duo passionate about flying. What started as a simple hobby gave us plenty of learning opportunity and great connections. We interact, learn from and work closely with a lot of professionals in this field including pilots, aeronautical engineers, professors and senior managers. Now my child flies a variety of models (quads, electric fixed wing planes, nitro planes...) and flies many routes on the flight simulator. We constantly participate in college projects and MIT (Chennai) helped us realize that a kid so young can compete at par with other engineers. Children have so much potential that we as parents have to use our time/resource as the key to unlock them.

Our interests include RC Flying, Aero-Modelling and Flight Simulation. We envision spreading this interest to others and more specifically young minds. We are a regular visitor at Aero India and my son is a big fan of Surya Kiran. We do not miss a single opportunity to learn something new about flying. We regularly keep connecting with professionals and organizations in aeronautics industry.
